CSS Measurement Units

Home

CSS measurement units can be split up into two different categories. They are relative and absolute.

Relative

  • em
  • ex
  • ch
  • rem
  • vw
  • vh
  • vmin
  • vmax
  • %

Relative units are recommended because they can easily be scaled between different devices.

Absolute

  • cm
  • mm
  • in
  • px
  • pt
  • pc

Absolute units are fixed. They do not change.
