Line

A line is the form of connection between two points.

What is the first type of line that comes to mind? It's probably straight. It doesn't matter whether it is horizontal, vertical, or diagonal. It is still just a typical, ordinary, boring line. Lines control the viewer's eye. Don't just draw lines to draw a line. Always make sure that each part of the composition, no matter how minuscule it may seem, has a purpose.

"Art is freedom. Being able to bend things most people see as a straight line." - Anonymous
