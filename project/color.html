Color

A color (hue) is the visual perception that enables one to differentiate otherwise identical objects.

The element of color is such a huge topic that it really can't be discussed and understood all at one time. There is just something in the human soul that can distinctly tell when something is visually pleasing...and when it is not. Your art will look and feel so much better when effectively using colors together.

"Color is a matter of taste and of sensitivity." - Edouard Manet
